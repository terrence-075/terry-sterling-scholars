About Terry Sterling Scholars

Excellence in Education Since 2018

Our Story

Terry Sterling Scholars Tuition Center was founded in 2018 in Francistown with a clear vision: to provide high-quality, affordable, and results-driven academic support to students in Botswana.

Named after the founder’s passion for sterling academic excellence and scholarly discipline, we started with just 25 students in a small classroom. Today, we proudly serve over 500 students annually and have helped hundreds achieve outstanding results in PSLE, JCE, BGCSE, and IGCSE examinations.

Our Mission

To unlock every student’s academic potential by providing personalized attention, modern teaching methods, and a supportive learning environment that nurtures confidence, discipline, and a love for learning.

Our Vision

To become the leading tuition center in Francistown and Northern Botswana, recognized for producing consistent top performers and well-rounded students who excel both academically and in character.

Why Choose Us?

  • Qualified & Experienced Teachers – All our tutors are graduates with proven teaching records.
  • Small Class Sizes – Maximum of 15 students per class for individualized attention.
  • Exam-Focused Curriculum – We follow the Botswana national syllabus and Cambridge IGCSE with extra exam practice.
  • Regular Progress Reports – Parents receive monthly feedback on their child’s performance.
  • Affordable Fees – Quality education without breaking the bank.
  • Convenient Location – Easily accessible at Minestone, Behind Galo Mall, Francistown.
