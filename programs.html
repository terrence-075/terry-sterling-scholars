Our Academic Programs

Structured, result-oriented tuition for every level

Choose the Right Program for Your Child

We offer comprehensive tuition across all major levels in the Botswana education system and Cambridge curriculum.

Primary School Leaving Examination (PSLE)

Target: Standard 5 – 7 Students

  • Mathematics
  • English
  • Science
  • Setswana

Focus: Building strong foundations and exam techniques.

Fees: P850 per month (3 subjects) | P1,100 per month (4+ subjects)

Junior Certificate Examination (JCE)

Target: Form 1 – 3 Students

  • Mathematics
  • English
  • Science (Integrated)
  • Social Studies & Agriculture

Focus: Transition support and concept mastery.

Fees: P950 per month (3 subjects) | P1,250 per month (4+ subjects)

Botswana General Certificate of Secondary Education (BGCSE)

Target: Form 4 & 5 Students

  • Core Subjects (Maths, English, Science)
  • Optional Subjects Support
  • Intensive Exam Preparation
  • Past Paper Practice & Revision

Focus: High performance in final national examinations.

Fees: P1,200 per month (3 subjects) | P1,600 per month (Full Package)

Cambridge IGCSE

Target: International Curriculum Students

  • Mathematics (Core & Extended)
  • English as a Second Language
  • Biology, Chemistry & Physics
  • Business Studies & Accounting

Focus: Cambridge syllabus coverage and exam excellence.

Fees: P1,400 per month
